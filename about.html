Our story

Built for people who just got their first salary.

Most personal finance content in India is written by people who already have wealth — for people who already have wealth. Charts about portfolio rebalancing when you don't even know what SIP stands for. Advice about diversification when you're trying to figure out why you got ₹38,000 when your CTC said ₹50,000.

RupeeSmart exists to fix that gap. We write about money the way a knowledgeable friend would explain it — clearly, honestly, without trying to sell you anything.

Every article on this site starts from the same place: you just got your first paycheck. Now what?
